About Us

The Note is a center for all your music needs! Whether it’s instruments, music, or merchandise from your favorite artists, we have it! We are a local operated business and we truly care about all of your musical needs. We will always do our best to get you what you are looking for! We look forward to doing business with you!As a company made up of musicians and music lovers, we're driven by our shared belief that music is the greatest unifying force in the world. And that idea fuels our mission to fill the world with music. To give people the tools they need to create and connect. To be seen and heard. And recently, to help and to heal. Music can stir the soul and change the frequency of the planet. So let's make some noise. Because the world needs to hear what you have to play..
